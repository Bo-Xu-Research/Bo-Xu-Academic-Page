Bo Xu (徐博)

I am the head of innovative product at JD.com AI Research where I work with IEEE fellow Dr. Xiaodong He and IEEE fellow Dr. Tao Mei on platform governance, content moderation and smart manufacturing. Before that, I worked as a Venture Capitalist for a major PE fund spinoff in Washington DC. For my education degrees, I finished my Master's at McIntire School of Commerce, University of Virginia, where I was advised by Bank of America Eminent Scholar Emeritus Prof. Thomas S. Bateman. I did my Bachelor of Science in Consumer Economics @ University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign where I worked as undergraduate research assistant for Prof. Ximing Cai and Prof. Xiang Liu.
